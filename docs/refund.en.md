# REFUND POLICY – FACTCHECKER AI

**Last updated:** March 2026

---

## Overview

All payments for points on the FactChecker AI platform are processed exclusively through **Lemon Squeezy**, who acts as the Merchant of Record for all transactions. Payments are handled directly by Lemon Squeezy in accordance with their terms of service and privacy policy.

---

## When a Refund is Offered

The operator offers a refund **only in the case of a proven technical error** where:

- the payment was successfully processed and confirmed by Lemon Squeezy, **but**
- the purchased points were **not credited** to the user's account within 24 hours.

---

## How to Submit a Request

If you believe you are facing the issue described above:

1. Contact us at **factcheckerai42@gmail.com** within **7 days** of the purchase date.
2. Provide the email address of your account and the payment confirmation from Lemon Squeezy.
3. We will verify the transaction and respond within **24 hours**.
4. If a technical error is confirmed, we will credit the points immediately or coordinate with Lemon Squeezy for a full or partial refund, depending on the circumstances.

---

## When a Refund is Not Offered

Refunds are **not offered** in the following cases:

- The points have already been **consumed** (used for analysis).
- The user has **changed their mind** after a successful purchase.
- The analysis results do not meet the user's expectations (the service is provided "as is").
- The account has been closed or access restricted due to a violation of the Terms and Conditions.

---

## Value of Points

Points have no monetary value outside the Platform and cannot be exchanged for money, transferred between accounts, or exported in any form.
